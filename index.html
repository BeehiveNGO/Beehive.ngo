Beehive is an open-source initiative launched by former United Nations staff bringing humanitarian organizations, tech volunteers and supporters together to work on vastly improving Humanitarian Crowdfunding.





Beehive team members are solving and modeling how to build, power and operate next-generation, intelligent, open-data-driven humanitarian crowdfunding applications vitally useful to humanitarian actors: more




Beehive is focusing on setting up a reliable power source developers can use to plug enterprise-grade applications into streaming aid activity data reported in compliance with the IATI Standard: more




Next, Beehive is building a humanitarian crowdfunding platform to solve and model how advanced new IATI-data-driven applications can run, function and optimally serve the humanitarian community: more




Beehive is also developing datasets and the knowhow needed to train artificial intelligent applications how to process aid activity information to answer queries about operations informing giving: more
